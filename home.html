Motivation

Revolutionizing Waste: The Urgency of Recycling for a Sustainable Future The global waste crisis is an escalating threat to our environment, public health, and economic stability, with Africa facing immense challenges in effective waste management. However, recycling presents a tremendous opportunity to turn this crisis into a sustainable future.

The upcoming African Recycling Initiative Conference, set for November in Accra, Ghana, will convene policymakers, environmental experts, business leaders, and community activists to discuss innovative solutions and policies that can drive impactful change across the continent. Attendees will gain insights from industry pioneers, explore cutting-edge recycling technologies, network with key stakeholders, and contribute to transformative sustainability initiatives.

Recycling is no longer an option—it is essential. By working together, we can convert waste into valuable resources, create green jobs, and preserve our environment. Join us in Accra this November to be part of the change for a cleaner, greener Africa.

About 1st AFRICA 2025

The global waste crisis is reaching alarming levels, with millions of tons of plastic, electronic, and organic waste polluting our environment every year. Africa, like the rest of the world, faces a growing challenge in managing waste effectively, threatening our ecosystems, public health, and economic potential. However, within this challenge lies an incredible opportunity—recycling.


The upcoming Recycling for a Sustainable African Future Conference, scheduled to take place in Accra Ghana this November, aims to be a pivotal event in the movement toward a cleaner, more sustainable Africa. This conference will bring together policymakers, environmental experts, business leaders, innovators, and community activists to explore practical solutions, innovative recycling technologies, and policies that can drive significant change in waste management across the continent.

Venue

Kempinski Hotel was selected as the venue for our conference in November 2025. Its central location in Accra provided an intimate and inclusive environment, easily accessible within walking distance from the International Conference Accra.

As an inclusive establishment, Kempinski Hotel offers a convenient grand opening arena and a welcoming, comfortable stay. Its urban forestry features enhance the venue, reflecting Accra’s vibrant aesthetic and commitment to climate change efforts.

Its spacious, well-equipped facilities will be fully utilized for networking and knowledge-sharing activities, fostering meaningful connections among the conference participants.
